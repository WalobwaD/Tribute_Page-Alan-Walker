ALAN WALKER
TRIBUTE

Alan Olav Walker is a British-born Norwegian DJ, YouTuber and record producer primarily known for the critically acclaimed single "Faded" (2015), which was certified platinum in 14 countries!

  • 1997: On the 24th of August, a star was born! He was raised in Northampton, England. His father (Philip Alan Walker), is of British origin while his mother (Hilde Omdal Walker), is of Norwegian origin. He has an elder sister Camila Joy and a younger brother, Andreas.

  • 2012: Growing up Alan developed a passion in music, he was also inclined towards graphic designing. In 2012, he listened to a song by David Whistle and contacted him about how he made his music. And that's hoe he became more interested in music. Although he did not have formal training, Walker learnt through youtube tutorials.

  • 2014: In August, he released his track, 'Fade' with the record label 'NoCopyRightSounds.' The track became immensely popular. It has over 300 million views on ‘YouTube,’ 70 million plays on ‘Spotify,’ and 20 million streams on ‘SoundCloud.’

  • 2015: He released a single track 'Faded' with 'MER Muskik' music production company, a remastered version of his earlier track which became more popular than the original song. It featured the Norwegian pop singer Iselin Solheim with over 1.9 billion views on Youtube and became top 10 most liked videos on Youtube.

  • 2017: He released the instrumental version of the song, 'Ígnite' featuring Norwegian music producer K-391. In May 2017, he recorded his first song with Irish singer Gavin James. The song was titled ‘Tired.’ Walker described it as a song that “adds another dimension to his productions. In October 2017, Walker released the song, ‘All Falls Down,’ featuring American singer, Noah Cyrus, and British record producer ‘Digital Farm Animals.’

  • 2018: Walker became the top ‘YouTuber’ in Norway, with more than 15 million subscribers. In May 2018, Walker, together with K-391, released the vocal version of the song, ‘Ignite,’ which featured Norwegian singer Julie Bergan, and South Korean singer Seungri. In July 2018, he released the song, ‘Darkside,’ featuring Au/Ra, and Tomine Harket.

Alan Walker has kept his personal life a closely-guarded matter. He is not married, and is not known to be in any relation. Though Walker is active on social media, he avoids disclosing his personal affairs. Walker is known for his unique attire during stage performances. He is always seen wearing a hoodie, and a mask that covers half of his face. Walker explains that he likes to maintain anonymity while being a public figure.
